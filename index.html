Taste Donut World is a pioneer brand in the donut industry in Kenya with currently three branches strategically located in Soin Arcade Westlands, 66 Mall Yaya centre and Gigiri. The company also opened its first restaurant in 2020 offering savoury meals.

We specialize in Donuts, the best you can have in town as our Chef has a secret ingredient, which together with our special donut mix and a special method to prepare makes our product stand out from our competitors. We also produce different types of cookies, muffins, cinnamon rolls, sausage rolls, meat pies, croissants, and Cakes
